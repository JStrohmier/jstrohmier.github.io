Access is for Everyone

Have you ever been unable to access a website that you were trying to reach? People with disabilities face issues like this regularly, and this is what I want to change.

Imagine trying to watch news broadcasted on a television at a loud restaurant or a busy airport without captions. Captions can be the difference between a good or bad experience for anyone accessing video content on your website.

I want to ensure a positive experience for your website users and prevent costly negative experiences.

With my degree in technology development and deployment, as well as my background in quantitative and qualitative research, I analyze issues that exist in websites and documents and I improve them. I want to maximize the potential for online content to be used universally.

I believe improving web access for everyone is the best way to connect your message to the world.

My name is Jean Strohmier and I help breach the gap between your content and your potential audience.

This website is under construction. I'm learning new things about web access and universal use every day.
