Heading Structure Training

The document in the left pane demonstrates a proper heading hierarchy. Headings should be nested in a logical order to communicate the structure of the content to all users, including screen reader users.

Heading Hierarchy in the Document

  • Foods I Like to Eat is the main topic of the document and is a Level 1 Heading (<h1>).
  • Fruits and Vegetables are major sections within the document and are Level 2 Headings (<h2>).
  • Berries is a subsection of Fruits and is a Level 3 Heading (<h3>).
  • The berry names (Strawberries, Raspberries, Blackberries, and Blueberries) are list items and are not headings.

Proper Nesting Example

H1  Foods I Like to Eat
├── H2 Fruits
│   └── H3 Berries
│       ├── H4 Strawberries
│       ├── H4 Raspberries
│       ├── H4 Blackberries
│       └── H4 Blueberries
└── H2 Vegetables

Heading levels should not be skipped. Since "Berries" belongs under "Fruits," it should be an H3 because it is one level below the H2 heading "Fruits."
