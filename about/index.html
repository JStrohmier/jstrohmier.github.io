I Wear Many Hats

Hello, I'm Jean (Lee) Strohmier. With my Master of Information Management degree and a background in psychology, I am especially interested in:

  • Overcoming barriers to access and usability of Internet resources.

  • The advancement of literacy initiatives through technology.

I've worked in research and writing copy in response to grant proposals, RFIs, an RFP, and other awards. Before that, I worked as a retail sales associate and ESOL tutor. I'm pretty good at:

  • Tutor = Sharing skills, tracking progress, and motivating growth.

Access First

Having access to information online is important for everyone. Up until fairly recently, organizations have approached web accessibility as a costly remediation effort when facing a discrimination lawsuit.

I want to help shift this focus from remediation to proactively being responsible and sustainable.
